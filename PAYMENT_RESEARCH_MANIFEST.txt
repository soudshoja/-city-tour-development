================================================================================
                         KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY
================================================================================

PAYMENT PATHS (4):
  1. Client Credit   → Immediate via PaymentApplicationService
  2. External Gateway → Redirect + webhook callback
  3. Cash            → Manual entry
  4. Split           → Credit + another gateway

CORE SERVICE:
  PaymentApplicationService::applyPaymentsToInvoice()
  ├─ 3 modes: full, partial, split
  ├─ Automatic: InvoicePartial, Credit, PaymentApplication, JournalEntry creation
  ├─ Transactional with rollback
  └─ Comprehensive logging

CORE MODELS (5):
  1. Invoice              → Status, payment tracking
  2. Payment             → Gateway payment records
  3. PaymentApplication  → Audit trail (NEW)
  4. InvoicePartial      → Split payment records
  5. Credit              → Balance tracking

PAYMENT GATEWAYS (5):
  1. MyFatoorah
  2. Tap
  3. Hesabe
  4. uPayment
  5. Knet

INVOICE STATUSES (6):
  • paid
  • unpaid
  • partial
  • paid by refund
  • refunded
  • partial refund

ACCOUNTING:
  • Automatic COA entries
  • Double-entry bookkeeping
  • DEBIT: Liabilities → Advances → Client → Payment Gateway
  • CREDIT: Accounts Receivable → Clients
